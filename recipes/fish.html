Fish

Description:

This blackened fish is cooked in a hot cast iron pan until charred and packed with flavor. This recipe calls for trout, but catfish or red snapper would be equally good. Be sure to have a well-ventilated kitchen!

Ingredients:

  • Paprika
  • Dry mustard
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Cumin
  • Black pepper
  • White pepper
  • Dried thyme
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Make the seasoning blend.
  2. Coat the fish in butter and season liberally.
  3. Cook each fish over high heat in melted butter until charred on both sides.
